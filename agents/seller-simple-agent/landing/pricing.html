Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ComplianceShield.

A scan is one compliance check against a single piece of content. This can be a blog post, social media caption, product description, or any text you submit. Pasting text and clicking "Scan" uses one scan credit. Submitting a YouTube URL also uses one scan credit, regardless of video length. Re-scanning the same content counts as a new scan.
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans support YouTube video scanning. Just paste the video URL and ComplianceShield will automatically extract the transcript, metadata, and description to check for compliance issues. We analyze the spoken content, title, description, and tags against applicable regulations like FTC disclosure requirements.
ComplianceShield checks against a comprehensive set of regulations relevant to content creators: FTC endorsement and disclosure guidelines, GDPR data privacy requirements, COPPA children's content rules, FDA health claims regulations, CAN-SPAM email marketing rules, and platform-specific policies. Enterprise customers can also add custom rules for their industry or brand guidelines.
No. ComplianceShield is an AI-powered tool that identifies potential compliance issues in your content. It is not a substitute for professional legal counsel. Our analysis highlights areas of concern and provides educational context, but you should consult a qualified attorney for legal advice specific to your situation. Think of it as a smart first pass that catches obvious issues before they become problems.
Enterprise plans include access to our MCP (Model Context Protocol) server and A2A (Agent-to-Agent) protocol support. This means AI agents like Claude, GPT, or your custom agents can call ComplianceShield as a tool to check content compliance programmatically. Agents discover our capabilities via the standard /.well-known/agent.json endpoint and make payment-protected requests through the Nevermined protocol. This enables automated compliance checking in your content pipeline.
