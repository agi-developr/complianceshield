# ComplianceShield - Business Brief

> AI-powered legal compliance checker for content creators.
> Hackathon 2026 | One-pager reference

## The Problem

Content creators operate in a minefield of FTC, ASA, and platform-specific disclosure rules. A single non-compliant post can trigger **$53,088+ in fines per violation** (FTC 2025 penalty rate). In 2024 alone, FTC enforcement returned **$337.3M to consumers** from disclosure violations. The FTC put **670 companies on notice** in 2023, and class-action lawsuits against influencers (Celsius, Shein) are accelerating in 2025-2026.

**Today's options are terrible:**
- **Lawyers**: $150-$1,000/hr ($313/hr average, 2025). A single content review costs $500-1,500.
- **CreatorIQ**: Enterprise-only, $30K-$90K/yr. Built for brands, not individual creators.
- **Lumanu**: Starts ~$99/mo but focuses on payments/tax compliance, not content review.
- **Manual checklists**: Error-prone, don't scale, can't keep up with rule changes.

**The gap: No affordable, AI-powered tool exists that scans creator content for legal compliance issues before publishing.**
